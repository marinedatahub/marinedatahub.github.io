Alkalinity Analysis


Technician George Anderson explains the analysis of seawater alkalinity samples. Alkalinity is one of the key ocean carbon parameters measured at sea, along with “dissolved inorganic carbon” (also known as total dissolved carbon), which is measured by another shipboard team. Thousands of these samples are run at sea each cruise.
