Rosette Recovery, Night, Rough


This video of a rosette recovery during high winds at night from the “I8S” cruise shows how difficult it can be to work in these conditions. Although the recovery were OK, there was some damage to one or two of the rosette bottles when the rosette hit the side of the ship. We had several recoveries under conditions like this during the “I8S” cruise.
