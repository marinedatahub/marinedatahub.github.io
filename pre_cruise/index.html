Pre-Cruise

During January 2006 we learned that the I8S cruise was scheduled for early 2007. Then ensued a year of final planning, putting together the shipboard team, obtaining research clearances, and purchasing and shipping research supplies. The documents accessed from this page provide a look at the initial research proposal itself and others produced during pre-cruise planning.
